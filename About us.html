About Meshatz

Meshatz is a platform for everyone to read, write and learn. The idea behind Meshatz was to create a platform that enables everyone to download about a particular topic of their expertise and help others learn.

Vision

Our vision is to create a hub for diverse, informative content that educates and inspires.

Goal

Bridging the knowledge gap by providing a platform that empowers people from all backgrounds to share their stories and perspectives, while also providing high-quality, informative content that helps readers better understand the world around them.


To reate a space where everyone can learn and grow, by providing a platform that combines diverse perspectives and high-quality information to help readers expand their understanding of the world.


Cultivating lifelong learnerning by creating a platform that inspires curiosity and encourages lifelong learning, by providing a space where people can explore new ideas, perspectives, and areas of interest in a supportive and inclusive community. Technologies


Meshatz is built with this amazing javascript libraries that are ReactJs and NodeJs. We also make use of firebase realtime database, firestore and firebase storage. Our amazing search bar is powered by Algolia-search.


We make use of customised Quill Editor supporting various features and instant preview of the content. This feature in not available on any other platform available. try it out
